The Second Realm

Purgatory, or The Other Place, is the second realm of the Madness Combat Infernolly lore. Purgatory is a supermassive mountain of cleansing and purification. The goal of Purgatory is to cleanse souls of their sins and permit them into Heaven.

There are nine layers to the mountain, each having its specific punishment. Once reaching Eden, the highest layer, Shades will wait to be transported to Heaven. Rumors speculate that Eden is now a town due to the disappearance of an angel.

The Other Place

Cruelty, or The Other Place is one of the lowest layers of Purgatory, and no one knows what lies beneath. Souls are placed in a dark red hellscape where they fight their worst fears. Every injury cuts a part of their physical body, and they will be consumed by Purgatory and morphed into Shades.

Punishment: The environment is purely based on life experiences, you cannot spot other individuals in this layer as it is a personalized hell.

Danger Level: High

Pride

Pride is the first layer of Purgatory Proper. It is a large plane of boulders and marble rock with a shiny gold color inside. Due to the invention of a Demon Deterrent System (DDS), a city has emerged in the center, where a small group of people hold power over others.

Punishment: Souls are forced by demons to carry heavy marble rock uphill to where they can cut them up or carve them into statues. A lot of the architecture of the other layers comes from Pride.

Danger Level: Low

Envy

Envy is a dark blue cavern of blind, lost spirits. All souls in Envy are stripped of their eyesight and forced to endlessly wander through the crag.

Some shades were spotted harming other souls for using a cane made out of stalagmite. After stealing the cane, they then proceeded to use it as a blind cane, proving to us researchers how much the sin of Envy affects people.

Punishment: Those in Envy are punished by having most water taken away from them. Upon reaching for a pool of water, it disappears in their hands, punishing them for their envy.

Danger Level: Medium

Asperity

Asperity is a large network of hills and troughs, each filled with a dark smoke that sinners much wander through endlessly. The wrathful reside here, punished for their hostility towards others. Despite this, a city emerged from the smoke, The Hanging City of Asperity. The city is locked off to most shades, as the leader only allows loyal servants.

Asperity is currently at war with Sloth, the layer above. Many soldiers were rumored to have gone up to Sloth, but never came out the other side.

Punishment: Constantly fight through the black fog to get to the top.

Danger Level: Medium

City Danger: Very High (unless you are accepted)
